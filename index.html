About Ruchi Tarangam

Welcome to Ruchi Tarangam (Waves of Flavors)! We are dedicated to bringing the pure essence of traditional, completely authentic home-cooked flavors right to your doorstep.

Every single batch of our rich native pickles, aromatic podis, delicious heritage sweets, and crisp snacks are prepared meticulously using recipes passed down through generations. We hold deep pride in our promise: Zero Added Preservatives, Zero Industrial Chemicals, Zero Artificial Colors, and completely Free from Palm Oil.

Taste the warmth, care, and quality of premium ingredients packed in every order!
